NOTATION USED:
B or b  Bend
B or b (17)  bend to appropriate tone, in this case tone of 17th fret
R or r   release bend
15b(17)r(17)15   bend string at 15th fret to get 17th fret tone then
release bend from 17th fret tone to 15th P or p  pull off
H or h Hammer on
Ah  Artificial Harmonics
This is not written to time, you should listen to the song and get the
feel of the notes and correlate them to the music.  Whammy bar and vibrato
have been left out.  You can hear where it is used.
