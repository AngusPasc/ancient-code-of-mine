How do I rent a scooter?
      1.Book your session and agree on pick-up location, call +421 949 178 
      789, +421 903 264 949 or send an email to rezervacie@pozicajsiskuter.sk
      2.Pick-up the scooter on agreed time and location, bring your driver's licence and one other photo ID (standard driver's licence is sufficient for operating 50 cmm scooters)
      3.Sign the rental contract
      4.You will be given all neccessary information and instructions including emergency contact
      5.We will provide you with helmet and of course all papers of evidence
      6.Return scooter on agreed time and location
