Coagulants Dosing Monitor

Instrument designed to control dosing of coagulants in water treatment plants. It is using the phenomenon, that reaction between coagulants and particles in water results in formation of polar particles. Turbidity particles have a negative charge and coagulant particles a positive one. The instrument responds to change of potential and keeps the set ratio of coagulants with regard to pollution of input water.

Instruments for indoors air quality and working enviroment measurement

MODEL 8525 P-TRAKÔ - Monitor of ultrafine particulates is a portable instrument detecting and counting ultrafine particulates (< 0.1 m m). For particulates detection patented principle of measurement is used - saturation of drawn sample gas by isopropyl alcohol vapour. During consequent condensation of alcohol vapour droplets of alcohol envelop particulates, which increase their volume and are easier detectable. Droplets pass further through a focussed laser beam causing "flashes" of light reflection sensed by the photodetector. Amount of particulates is calculated from the number of light flashes captured on the detector.

P-TrakÔ has no equivalent all over the world - it is the only particulates counter capable to detect particulates with diameter 0.02 m m to 1 m m. Typical measuring range is 0 ¸ 5 x 105 particulates/cm3.

DustTRAKÔ is designed for particulates in air mass concentration measurement in real time. For particulates detection it uses known principle of light reflection from solid particulates sensed by the photodetector placed in angle 90° towards laser beam. Built-in pump draws sample through a measuring chamber. Protecting sheath air system isolates aerosols in the chamber, thus avoiding optics contamination, increasing reliability and reducing instrument maintenance requirements. Measuring range is 0.001 to 100 mg/m3.

Q-TRAK 

measures simultaneously key indoors air quality parameters: CO2, CO, temperature and humidity. CO2 sensor works on NDIR and CO sensor on electrochemical principle of measurement.

Both sensors are simply to calibrate directly at a measuring site. Built-in datalogger allows to store data into internal memory and following connection to PC their evaluation and archiving using TRAK-PROÔ Data software supplied with the instrument.

Personal particulates sampler RESPICONÔ .

For measurement of dust concentration in work-places it is necessary according to the European standard EN181 to provide for measurement of three dust size fractions:

  • breathable fractions
  • thoraidal fractions
  • alveolar fractions

Personal sampler RESPICONÔ allows to measure all of these fractions. Instrument is very easy to operate. Particulates separation according to size is provided by two partial separators and particulates are captured on three filters being weighed before and after sampling. Filters can be naturally analysed chemically as well as using microscope. Air flow through the instrument is provided by a battery operated pump with regulation. 

Unusual application of TSI technology represent PORTACOUNT series instruments designed for filters efficiency and correct application of breathing masks and respirators monitoring. Instrument working principle resides in an accurate particulates number monitoring in ambient air and internal space of the mask. These instruments follow recent trends in legislation development in the field of health in work-place protection.
